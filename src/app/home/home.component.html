What Makes Fako Extraordinary

Nestled at the foot of Mount Fako — an awe-inspiring active volcano rising above 4,000 m — our cultural center thrives in a region where ancient traditions meet modern innovation. Mount Fako, revered by the Bakweri as “Mongo ma Ndemi” (the Mountain of Greatness), is not only a spiritual symbol, but also a living monument of identity and endurance.

Echoing this powerful legacy, the town of Buea and surrounding areas have become known as “Silicon Mountain” — a booming tech ecosystem that plays on our volcanic heritage and mirrors Silicon Valley’s spirit of creativity and progress.

Here at FMCC, we embrace this union of tradition and innovation. We celebrate the mountain’s storied heritage and nurture the rising waves of youth-led tech, art, and cultural revival — building a future that’s rooted in our past, yet soaring toward tomorrow.

Building the Future of Fako

The Fako Multipurpose Cultural Center, FMCC is envisioned to be a beacon of culture, innovation, and unity — a home where the heartbeat of tradition meets the rhythm of tomorrow. This visionary project will rise at the foot of the majestic Mount Fako, welcoming generations to learn, create, and celebrate together.

The FMCC complex will feature a grand performance hall, interactive heritage museum, modern conference spaces, and creative studios — all designed to empower our community and share our story with the world.

But this dream will only become reality with the power of our people. Every donation — big or small — becomes a brick in our foundation, a light in our corridors, and a melody in our halls. Together, we will raise walls that speak of our pride and pave paths that lead into the future.

Be part of history — help us build the heart of Fako!
